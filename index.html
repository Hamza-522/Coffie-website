Our History

Whether you're searching for something new to warm your mug, seeking the best brew method for your favorite blend or exploring our rarest offerings, you’ve come to the right place.

ur story begins in 1971 along the cobblestone streets of Seattle’s historic Pike Place Market. It was here where Starbucks opened its first store, offering fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. Our name was inspired by the classic tale, “Moby-Dick,” evoking the seafaring tradition of the early coffee traders.

We like to say that we are not in the coffee business serving people, but in the people business serving coffee. Our employees – who we call partners
